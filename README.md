## üö© Business Problem
The organization lacked a centralized reporting system to analyze sales and customer data.  
Managers needed a clear view of:
- Overall revenue and order performance trends
- Top performing products and there sales data.
- Top customers & high-value opportunities
- Detailed customer segmentation for targeted decision-making

---

## üéØ Project Objectives
- Track total revenue, profit, orders, and return rate trends
- Identify high-performing and low-performing products
- Provide insights into customer groups and purchase behavior
- Allow executives to analyze performance across time ranges and categories
- Enable product-level pricing simulations to estimate financial impact

- Revenue increased significantly in 2022 driven by bike accessories & tires category.
